FantasyDJ

Welcome to FantasyDJ!

FantasyDJ is a mobile app that allows users to team up and create playlists to compete each week. Using a user's Spotify account, playlists are scored based on daily changes in song popularity.

Learning to play is simple!

In a few short steps, you and a friend can be on your way to predicting hits!

Start a League

To start a league, press the "Start New League" button at the bottom of the screen.

Name your League and Choose Your Opponent

Name your league whatever you like. Invite your opponent using their Spotify ID.

Choose Your Songs

Take turns choosing songs for your league.

Pick Your Songs from Spotify

Pick your songs from Spotify's vast catalog of tunes.

Sit Tight (or Fret Nervously)

Over the next week, you can track songs' progress using the analytics page.

Be a Winner (or Not)

At the end of the week, the user whose songs gained the most popularity overall wins.

Have Fun!
