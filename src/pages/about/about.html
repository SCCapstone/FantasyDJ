About FantasyDJ Welcome to FantasyDJ!

FantasyDJ is a mobile application that allows users to team up and create playlists to compete each week, much like fantasy football. Utilizing a user's Spotify account, playlists are scored based upon plays worldwide.

Learning to play is simple!

All you have to do is find the "create a league" button on the home tab and add another player. From there, you can pick a song that you think will become even more popular in the coming week and add it to your playlist. After this, your opponent will be allowed to add a song, and you will go back and forth.
